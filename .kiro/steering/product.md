# Employee QR Track - Product Overview

## What is Employee QR Track?

Employee QR Track is a modern employee attendance and management system that uses QR codes for quick and secure check-ins. The platform provides a comprehensive solution for businesses to track employee attendance, manage shifts, and generate reports.

## Key Features

- **QR Code Check-in/Check-out**: Fast and contactless employee attendance tracking
- **Multi-Device Support**: Works on mobile devices and kiosk terminals
- **Real-time Dashboard**: Live attendance monitoring and analytics
- **Employee Management**: Complete employee profiles and role management
- **Shift Scheduling**: Plan and manage employee work schedules
- **Reporting**: Generate attendance reports and analytics
- **Multi-tenant**: Support for multiple companies with role-based access

## User Roles

- **Owner**: Company owner with full access
- **Admin**: Administrative access to company data
- **Manager**: Manage employees and view reports
- **Employee**: Check-in/out and view personal attendance
- **Kiosk**: Dedicated terminal mode for shared check-in points

## Target Users

- Small to medium businesses
- Retail stores
- Restaurants and cafes
- Service companies
- Any business needing employee time tracking

## Business Model

- **Free Plan**: Basic features for small teams
- **Pro Plan**: Advanced features and analytics for growing businesses
